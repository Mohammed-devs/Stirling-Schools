Extracurricular Activities

Explore passions, develop talents, and build friendships beyond the classroom.

Learning and Growth Beyond the Classroom

Stirling Schools offers a rich tapestry of extracurricular activities designed to complement academic learning. These programs provide students with opportunities to explore diverse interests, hone new skills, develop leadership qualities, and foster teamwork.

A World of Opportunities

We offer a diverse range of activities to cater to every student's passion.

Sports & Athletics

From team sports like basketball and soccer to individual pursuits, our athletics program promotes physical fitness, discipline, and sportsmanship.

  • Basketball Teams
  • Soccer Leagues
  • Volleyball
  • Track & Field
  • Swimming Club

Arts & Culture

Unleash creativity through our diverse arts programs, including visual arts, drama, music, and cultural showcases.

  • Drama Club & Productions
  • School Band & Choir
  • Art & Craft Workshops
  • Photography Club
  • Cultural Exchange Programs

Academic & STEM

Challenge yourself and expand your knowledge with academic competitions, STEM projects, and intellectual pursuits.

  • Debate Team
  • Math Olympiad
  • Science Fair
  • Robotics Club
  • Coding Challenges

Featured Activities

Highlighting some of our most popular and impactful programs.

Model United Nations (MUN)

Students develop skills in diplomacy, public speaking, and international relations by simulating UN committees and debating global issues.

Robotics & Coding Challenges

Hands-on experience in designing, building, and programming robots, participating in competitions and fostering innovation.

Ready to Explore?

Getting involved is easy! Information about activity sign-ups, schedules, and new offerings is regularly shared through school newsletters and student portals. We encourage all students to find an activity that sparks their interest.
