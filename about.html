About Us

Learn more about Stirling Schools, our mission, vision, and our commitment to educational excellence.

Our Story

Stirling Schools provide the best education services by bringing professional educators together and investing continuously in educational technologies and facilities.

Our schools prepare individuals to understand, contribute to, and succeed in a rapidly changing society, thus making the world a better and more just place. Within our strategic planning, there is a strong focus on digital learning, ensuring that we provide a strong digital curriculum, supported by excellent teaching and access to the latest technology.

The Stirling Schools group, established for over 27 years in Iraq, has been successfully operating private and international schools across the country for nearly a quarter of a century. It offers high-quality education, digitally supported by a variety of educational facilities. All of our schools are licensed and authorized by the Ministry of Education and have international accreditation.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to build nurturing, supportive and collaborative communities of learners by combining local cultural values with international standards of education.

Our Vision

Our vision is to inspire and empower our students to become global citizens with a deeply ingrained sense of social responsibility and creativity, readily equipped with personal qualities such as a fully rounded, independent character, and developed critical thinking skills so that they are imbued with the ability to unlock their full academic potential and exercise their talents in real life.

We recognize that outstanding academic results occur best when a student's emotional, social and physical well-being is developed alongside a strong work ethic. We focus in depth on the use of the latest digital innovations and the most advanced technology in order to maximize the content delivery offered by our educators to our learners.

"Our goal is to develop independent, confident, respectful, and resilient individuals with strong ethical values."

Philosophy

As befits a global provider of affordable education, we deliver our lessons for the most part in English. We set the highest levels of accountability for ourselves, ensuring optimum standards of achievement for all.

We aim to acknowledge and create opportunities for personal and spiritual development and for social and moral growth. We provide engaging learning pathways in order to equip our pupils, our students, and our staff with the necessary tools to unlock their full potential.

Why Choose Us

We have implemented a coherent education model globally with a strong digital curriculum, supported by excellent teaching and the latest technology. Our strength is in providing dynamic and engaging English language curricula in classrooms across Iraq.

  • Internationally accredited by ASIC and City & Guilds
  • Strong digital curriculum and modern technology
  • Multinational and multilingual student body
  • Experienced expatriate and local teachers
  • Focus on character development and universal values

Our Schools

Stirling Schools operates both international and private schools across Iraq.

International Schools

The Stirling Schools group operates two international schools located in Erbil and Sulaymaniyah. The UK-based quality assurance bodies ASIC and City & Guilds have accredited our international schools.

English is the medium of instruction across all subjects except for mother tongue languages and foreign language classes. Our teaching staff are in the main expatriate teachers.

Our international schools offer an internationally accredited program that equips students with internationally recognised academic skills through a rigorous curriculum based on balanced learning standards and complemented by a developed program of fruitful co-curricular and extra-curricular activities.

Locations:

Erbil Sulaymaniyah

Private Schools

The Stirling Schools group operates 44 private schools from Kindergarten to High School level. The medium of instruction for English, Maths, Science, and Computer is the English language while other subjects are delivered in Arabic and Kurdish.

Our educational staff consists of both expatriates and local teachers. All of our schools are licensed and authorised by the Ministry of Education and have international accreditation.

Locations:

Erbil Soran Sulaymaniyah Halabja Duhok Baghdad Kirkuk Basrah

Accreditations & Partnerships

Stirling Schools are accredited by reputable international bodies and collaborate with world-class educational institutions.

Our Accreditations

City & Guilds Accreditation

Stirling Schools' Education Quality is endorsed by City & Guilds, one of the world's most reputable and prominent British organizations. Our students receive an international certificate of completion. Centre no: 847237

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

Stirling Schools is quality assured by ISO for its administration and management, ensuring high standards in our educational provisions.

ASIC Accreditation

Our international schools are accredited by the Accreditation Service for International Schools, Colleges and Universities (ASIC), a UK-based quality assurance body recognized globally.

Our International Partners

Cambridge Educational Partnership

We collaborate with Cambridge University Press, purchasing authentic, original, and digitally supported course materials. Our teachers regularly receive training from Cambridge experts on teaching methodologies and educational topics.

Cambridge Assessment of English

Stirling Schools are centers authorized to run Cambridge YLE (Starters, Movers, Flyers), Key, Preliminary, and First exams. We offer these official exams and prepare candidates across our locations.

TOEFL Exam Centre

Our schools are exclusive authorized centers for TOEFL Primary Step 1, Step 2, Junior examinations, and TOEFL ITP exams for high school students, with preparation and testing centers across all our locations.

Stirling Schools Action Plan within the UNESCO Education 2030 Framework

We align our educational strategies and goals with the UN's 2030 framework for Education.

UNESCO Education 2030

"Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all."

How Stirling Schools Contributes to UNESCO's Goals

Quality Education & Gender Equality

We provide quality primary and secondary education with international partners offering equal provision for boys and girls. We offer scholarships with a higher percentage allocated to girls (positive discrimination).

Early Childhood Development

Our Kindergarten section offers access to quality pre-primary education, preparing boys and girls both academically and socially for primary education.

Technical & Vocational Skills

In collaboration with City & Guilds, we provide internationally recognized technical and vocational qualification programs and employability skills schemes across our schools.

Sustainable Development & Global Citizenship

Our Values Curriculum teaches human rights, gender equality, appreciation of differences, global citizenship, and cultural diversity. Our motto is 'World Peace through Personal Peace.'

Teacher Qualification

We've established a Teachers' Academy which is internationally accredited by City & Guilds, nurturing qualified teachers and providing training programs for all educational sectors.
